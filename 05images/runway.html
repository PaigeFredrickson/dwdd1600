Production Steps

Our runway pieces undergo a rigorous three-step creation process to maintain quality and ethics:

  1. Source recycled fabric: We hunt for high-quality remnants that would otherwise be discarded.
  2. Pattern making: Our designers use zero-waste cutting techniques to minimize scraps.
  3. Final assembly: Each piece is hand-sewn by master tailors in our NY studio.

Fashion Glossary

Organic Cotton
Cotton grown without harmful chemicals.
Recycled Polyester
Fabric made from plastic bottles.
Zero Waste
Design techniques that use every scrap of fabric.
