Who Is Paige Anyway?

Welcome to my creative space. I am a designer focused on the intersection of modern aesthetics and sustainable practices. My work explores how we can create high-fashion silhouettes using ethically sourced materials and zero-waste patterns.

The Vision

I believe that fashion should be a conversation between the wearer and the environment. By utilizing organic fibers and upcycled textiles, each piece tells a story of rebirth and intentionality.

Sustainable Materials

  • Hemp and Organic Cotton
  • Recycled Silk
  • Plant-based Leathers
  • Upcycled Denim
